# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises, including the accountability updates from issue #9.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers define user journeys, interaction patterns, and interface designs so teams can deliver intuitive, accessible experiences.

### Responsibilities
- Run or support user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and interaction guidance
- Align design decisions to product goals and success metrics
- Review implementations for usability and accessibility fit

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Partner with Product Managers to shape requirements and acceptance criteria
- Work with Developers during implementation to resolve design trade-offs
- Collaborate with Project Managers to plan design milestones and review checkpoints

---

## Scrum Masters / Agile Coaches

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters / Agile Coaches help teams apply agile ways of working, improve delivery flow, and remove process impediments.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Coach teams on agile practices and continuous improvement
- Surface and remove blockers that impact delivery
- Improve team collaboration, predictability, and accountability

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Work with Project Managers on delivery cadence, flow metrics, and dependencies
- Support Product Managers with backlog readiness and planning quality
- Help Developers and QA teams maintain sustainable execution practices

---

## Technical Writers

### Role Summary
Technical Writers ensure internal and external documentation stays accurate, understandable, and aligned to product changes.

### Responsibilities
- Produce and maintain user guides, release notes, and technical documentation
- Standardize documentation structure and quality across project artifacts
- Collaborate on documentation updates during feature delivery
- Support knowledge transfer for onboarding and operations

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Work with Developers to capture technical details and implementation notes
- Partner with Product Managers on audience needs and content priorities
- Coordinate with Customer Support Leads to keep help content current

---

## Security Leads

### Role Summary
Security Leads embed security practices across planning, implementation, and release to reduce risk and improve incident readiness.

### Responsibilities
- Lead threat modeling and security risk reviews
- Guide secure design and implementation decisions
- Track vulnerabilities and remediation priorities
- Support incident response planning and security communication

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Collaborate with Developers on secure coding and remediation actions
- Advise Product and Project Managers on risk acceptance and mitigation plans
- Coordinate with release and support stakeholders for security incident updates

---

## Customer Support Leads

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads represent post-release customer needs, operational feedback, and communication quality.

### Responsibilities
- Triage support trends and escalate recurring product issues
- Maintain support readiness for releases and known issues
- Provide customer-impact feedback to planning and prioritization
- Coordinate user-facing incident and release communication

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Partner with Product Managers to prioritize high-impact customer pain points
- Collaborate with Developers on reproducible cases and resolution details
- Work with Technical Writers to keep support and release documentation aligned
